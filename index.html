About Me

My name is John Filiaga. The Filiaga name comes from Samoa, although by looking at me, Samoan may be the last nationality you would guess. That's on account of me being mixed with Mexican, German, and English among various other ethnicites. I was born in Las Vegas, Nevada, but have lived most of my life here in Utah with a couple short stints in California. I graduated from Highland Highschool in Salt Lake City, and have spent some time attending Weber State University in Ogden. When I'm not working anywhere from 8-12 hours shifts at a Smith's warehouse, I'm usually playing games (Heroes of the Storm/Civ), playing guitar (pretty averagely), or just hanging out with friends and family. I enjoy most generic activities like reading, binge watching Netflix, sports (Jazz diehard), and all that other stuff. I'm an avid podcast listener which really comes in handy when trying to learn new things (like coding!). There's not a whole lot of special things about me, but I like to look at that as just me being well-rounded.
